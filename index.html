FAQ

Common questions.

Is MoveMate free for customers?
Yes, 100% free. You never pay to receive quotes. MoveMate is funded by professional partners who pay a small fee to receive qualified leads.
How are removalists and cleaners verified?
Every partner submits their ABN and current certificate of public liability insurance before being approved. We manually review all applications.
How fast will I receive quotes?
Most customers receive their first quote within 30-60 minutes. All partner businesses receive a real-time SMS alert the moment your request is submitted.
What does it cost to be a partner?
Partners start with 2 free leads. After that, leads are purchased in bundles from $10 per lead. No contracts, cancel any time.
Can I request quotes for multiple services at once?
Yes. Select Removals, Bond Cleaning and Storage together. Each request goes to the relevant specialist types in your city.
